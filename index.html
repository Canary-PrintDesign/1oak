Tell me more about 1oak

Tischlermeister Wolf Ehmanns, owner of 1oak, was born and raised in Germany. After starting his woodworking career in 1993, he received the Canadian equivalent of Red Seal Journeyman Tickets in both Carpentry and Joinery (cabinetry & furniture building).
He continued his woodworking education to achieve the German Masters degree in joinery, allowing him to provide high quality design, drafting, and production in all of your woodwork projects.

His work embodies the well-known German 'attention to detail'




Wolf owned and operated a profitable business in Germany for many years before he moved to Canada in 2007, when he started working in the commercial cabinetry industry. **Why move to Canada maybe?**
During his years in the commercial industry, he realized the need for a shop like 1oak – where custom cabinetry meets design in perfect harmony.
In 2011 Wolf moved to Nanoose Bay, BC and started the operation of 1oak.

His extensive training allows him to work with his clients to assess their needs, budget, and ideal vision - and implement the drafting, design and creation of custom cabinetry, furniture and interior construction and decoration. **consider revising**

The main passion at 1oak is on kitchens, bathrooms and unique solitary furniture but includes multi-trade renovation and interior projects as well. **Consider revising first part**

Everything we do is custom! We do it all in-house - from the design process to the finished product! There are no limitations. If you can imagine it, we can build it!

Cabinetry

Everything we do at 1oak hinges on classic joinery and furniture making.
It is where experience, innovation and craftsmanship go hand in hand and complete the cycle.

This is what we deal with since the founding days and look back on decades of experience.

Extravagant plans as well as standardized cabinet solutions are our daily tasks; only your imagination will be the limit.

Wood, plastics, glass, metal, composites; we transform all materials known to mankind into your dream fixtures for any living arrangements.

Display cases, Sideboards, TV-Multimedia-furniture, tables, interior or exterior benches and chairs are our repertoire as well as kitchens, wardrobes and shop fitting.
